Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This policy explains how the MeritOn Aptitude Platform collects and uses information when you access our website and participate in examinations.

Information we collect

  • Account data: name, university ID, email, department, and registration details you provide.
  • Login security data: login timestamp and IP address to protect accounts and maintain audit logs.
  • Examination data: answers, start/submit times, duration, and scores.
  • Integrity monitoring data: counts of tab switches and fullscreen exits during proctored sessions, used for malpractice review.

Why we collect it

  • Authenticate candidates and administrators
  • Deliver and grade examinations
  • Detect and review potential examination misconduct
  • Generate institutional reports and publish results
  • Maintain platform security and troubleshoot technical issues

Data storage & processing

Data is processed through Google Apps Script and stored in institutional Google Sheets/Drive resources controlled by your organization. Transport uses HTTPS (TLS). We do not sell personal data.

Retention

Records are retained according to your institution’s academic and examination policies. Contact your examination office for deletion requests where applicable.

Your rights

You may request correction of inaccurate profile data through your institution’s administrator. Monitoring during exams is disclosed before you begin a test.
