About MeritOn Aptitude Platform

Last updated: May 2026

MeritOn Aptitude Platform is an institutional computer-based testing solution designed for secure online examinations. It supports candidate authentication, scheduled tests, automated evaluation, administrator dashboards, and transparent result publication.

What we provide

  • Candidate registration, login, and examination lobby
  • Timed assessments with autosave and recovery
  • Administrative test creation, question management, and analytics
  • Integrity monitoring (tab visibility and fullscreen events) for fair examinations
  • Secure backend integration via Google Apps Script and institutional spreadsheets

Our commitment

We prioritize data minimization, HTTPS transport, and clear disclosure of monitoring practices. Examination data is used for academic and administrative purposes only.
